# Design System: Clinical Curator

## Core Philosophy
The design should feel like a premium medical editorial. Clean, sophisticated, and trustworthy. No hard lines; use tonal layering and subtle shadows to define sections.

## Colors
- **Primary**: `#00459a` (Deep Trust Blue)
- **Secondary**: `#006b5f` (Clinical Teal/Emerald)
- **Background**: `#f7f9fb` (Soft Neutral)
- **Surface**: `#ffffff` (Pure White)
- **Ink**: `#191c1e` (Near Black for readability)

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Plus Jakarta Sans`, font-weight: 800-900. Tight letter-spacing (-0.02em).
- **Body**: `Inter`, font-weight: 400-600. Optimized for medical readability.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded-full, high-end gradients for CTAs, ghost/outline for secondary actions.
- **Cards**: Large radius (2xl), deep soft shadows, no borders.
- **Glassmorphism**: Used for fixed elements like headers or floating badges.

## Layout
- **Tonal Layering**: Alternate sections between `--surface-lowest` (#ffffff) and `--surface-low` (#f2f4f6) or `--surface-container` (#eceef0) to create depth without lines.
- **Whitespace**: Generous padding (10rem+) between major sections.
